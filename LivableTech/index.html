From Livable Cities to Livable Tech

How to avoid turning the internet into a consumerist hell.

Many of us in tech want to change the game by which consumer technology succeeds or fails. We ask: under what circumstances would software compete to improve human lives, rather than to maximize screen "engagement" or to mediate relationships? We believe that by measuring success differently, by designing differently, and by changing how the platforms work, we can build tech that serves lives and relationships well. join us.
