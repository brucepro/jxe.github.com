Software that Serves Life

Joe Edelman // March 2015.

We need to change the game that software and media companies are playing with our lives. It's left us yoked to our devices, even when we'd rather not be. In particular, we need new rules for designing software and new rules for measuring its success.

If you'd like to help us redesign screens, reinvent analytics, or create better platform APIs, join the redefining software newsletter. We can show the world a new role for software to play in our lives.
