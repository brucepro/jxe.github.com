From Livable Cities to Livable Tech

Software should succeed by improving human lives, not by maximizing screen time or mediating relationships.

We are a community of 600+ professionals--designers, OS engineers, and product metrics people. We are reimagining interfaces, inventing new software success metrics, redesigning platform APIs, and changing how apps and OSes are marketed. Redesigning tech so as to serve lives and serve relationships well.
